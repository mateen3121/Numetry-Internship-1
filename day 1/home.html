Taj Mahal

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Taj Mahal is an iconic symbol of love. Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, it features stunning white marble architecture.

Mahabaleshwar Temple

Situated in the scenic hill station of Mahabaleshwar, this ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.

Thar Desert

The Thar Desert, also known as the Great Indian Desert, is a vast arid region in the northwestern part of India. It is known for its unique landscape and cultural heritage.

Ooty

Also known as Udhagamandalam, Ooty is a popular hill station in the Nilgiri Hills. It is renowned for its lush green landscapes, tea gardens, and pleasant climate.

Coorg

Known as the "Scotland of India," Coorg is famous for its coffee plantations, misty hills, and vibrant culture. It is a perfect destination for nature lovers.
